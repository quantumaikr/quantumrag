## Key Insights

The analysis demonstrates a tight race at the top between GPT-5.2 and Gemini 3.1 Pro, with only 0.08 points separating them on overall benchmark average. Claude Opus 4.6 holds third place but leads in safety benchmarks. Open-source models like DeepSeek V3.2 dominate the efficiency metric, offering 4x better performance-per-dollar than the leading proprietary models. The speed-accuracy tradeoff remains stark: the fastest models (Granite 3.3 8B at 387 tok/s) score significantly lower on benchmarks than slower flagships.
